English Translation:
I heard it.. the voice of the wind talk I want to know the ending of the dream I had Nobody wants to help me out with an answer My chest is stained entirely deep red... Oh, for you dear I want, want at least for you to understand Why I have to go back, I just need for you to know Oh how the forever reaching white mountain covered earth just seems so endless Although I scream at the top of my lungs nobody else can even hear me As if to try to hide this body's pain that I feel inside I am embraced now with kindness from the gentle sky Twinkling in your eyes, how the moonlight was so beautiful then Even if it would seem that the night is never done Oh how the forever reaching white mountain covered earth just seems so endless Although I scream at the top of my lungs nobody else can even hear me Oh how the forever reaching white mountain covered earth seems endlessly deep And now I'm falling again and this time there's not a single soul to save me As if to try to feel this body's warmth in its tiny frame I am embraced now with kindness from the lil' wagtail With the kindness now from the gentle sky I will sleep, I'll close my eyes I feel the warmth of the earth now too, it holds me...
